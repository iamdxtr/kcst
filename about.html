About Us

Welcome to Kalinga College of Science and Technology (KCST), a premier institution dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation. Located in the heart of Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR), KCST stands as a beacon of academic brilliance and holistic development. We empower students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical grounding required to excel in their chosen fields and contribute meaningfully to society.


Why Choose KCST?

  • Cutting-edge Curriculum: Designed to meet the demands of an ever-evolving world.
  • Experienced Faculty: A team of dedicated educators and industry experts.
  • State-of-the-art Facilities: Modern labs, libraries, and digital learning tools.
  • Career-oriented Programs: Tailored to prepare students for success in their professional journeys.
  • Student-Centric Approach: A supportive environment that encourages personal growth and development.

Since its inception in 2006, KCST has been a hub for academic excellence, producing graduates who excel in their careers and contribute to societal progress. Our alumni network spans diverse fields, reflecting our commitment to nurturing versatile and skilled professionals. At KCST, education goes beyond the classroom. We invite you to become part of a vibrant community where dreams take flight and innovation shapes the future. Together, let’s build a better tomorrow.
