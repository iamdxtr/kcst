History

Founded in 1982, Kalinga College of Science and Technology (KCST) emerged as a beacon of education in the heart of Kalinga. With a vision to nurture young minds and propel them towards excellence in science and technology, KCST began its journey with a humble yet determined spirit.

From its modest beginnings, KCST steadily expanded its academic offerings and infrastructure, striving to meet the evolving needs of students and the demands of a rapidly changing world. Through dedication and innovation, the college soon earned a reputation for academic excellence and cutting-edge research.

Over the years, KCST has remained committed to its mission of fostering a culture of learning, discovery, and innovation. Its vibrant campus has served as a melting pot of ideas, where students from diverse backgrounds come together to explore, create, and push the boundaries of knowledge.

As KCST continues to grow and evolve, it stands as a testament to the power of education to transform lives and shape the future. With each passing year, it reaffirms its commitment to excellence, serving as a beacon of hope and opportunity for generations to come.
